Music

Piano, Sax, & Clarinet

My love for music started in second grade, when I first starting taking piano lessons. I picked up the clarinet for band class in 4th grade, and the saxophone for jazz band in 7th grade. Throughout high school and college, I played in the wind ensemble at school, a regional jazz band, the pit orchestra for my high school's theater department, and even an 80's cover band with gigs at local bars. Music is a huge part of my life, and I hope to bring some of the creativity and beauty I've learned to my game development career.
